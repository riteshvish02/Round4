FAQ's

What is RunwayML Gen-3 Alpha?

Gen-3 Alpha is the newest version of RunwayML, offering advanced AI tools for generating creative content like images and videos. It’s currently available in early access.

How do I get access to Gen-3 Alpha?

You can request access by signing up on our website. Invitations are sent based on availability and your use case.

Are my Gen-2 projects compatible with Gen-3 Alpha?

Most Gen-2 projects should be compatible, but some features or workflows may differ, so it’s a good idea to review them after migrating.

What kind of support is available for Gen-3 Alpha users?

As a Gen-3 Alpha user, you’ll have access to our support team via email and live chat. We also provide extensive documentation, tutorials, and community forums

Can I upload my own AI models to RunwayML?

Yes, RunwayML allows you to upload and utilize your own AI models. You can also fine-tune pre-existing models available on the platform.
